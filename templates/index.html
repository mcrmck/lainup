Welcome to LAINUP

AI-Backed lineup generation

LAINUP will generate the optimal lineup for a given set of 9 players. Coaches can choose to either used the stat-based version, if they have appropriate records, or the ratings version. In which coaches rate their players from 0-99 in key categories. The algorithm behind the generation is adapted from the work of Mark D. Pankin's work, which can be found here.
